Disruption, Innovation and Change in Commercial Real Estate
Register Now
 

Workplace executives favor more varied and technologically sophisticated workspaces to meet the rising expectations employees have of their employers today. However, with the increase in team members working from home, employers are urging their staff to come in more often to the office, enticing them with state-of-the-art office space and services. How will these countervailing trends play out, and what does it mean for office property investors, owners and developers? Join leaders from AC Martin, Cushman & Wakefield, Hines and Ocean West as they discuss these trends and major challenges and opportunities presented by transportation constraints, emerging services and amenities, and shorter lease durations.

Also, don't miss Convene CEO Ryan Simonetti as he presents 5 Predictions on the Future of Office & Workspace as well as a special presentation on the Game-Changing Impacts of the Driverless Car with Gensler Co-CEO Andy Cohen, RETHINK: Emerging Trends in Real Estate on December 13 in Los Angeles.
